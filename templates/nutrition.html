Nutrition Tips

Fuel your body right to maximize your results

Why Nutrition Matters

No matter how hard you train, your results are heavily determined by what you eat. Nutrition accounts for roughly 70-80% of your physical transformation.

Protein — The Building Block of Muscle

Protein is essential for muscle repair and growth. After a workout, your muscles have tiny tears in the fibres — protein is what repairs and grows them back stronger.

Carbohydrates — Your Main Energy Source

Carbohydrates are your body's preferred fuel for exercise.

Hydration

Water is the most underrated performance enhancer. Drink at least 3 litres of water per day and more on training days.

Pre-Workout Nutrition

Eat a balanced meal 1.5 to 2 hours before training — containing carbohydrates for energy and moderate protein. Avoid heavy or fatty meals right before a workout as they slow digestion and make you sluggish.

Post-Workout Nutrition

The 30-60 minute window after a workout is when your muscles are most receptive to nutrients. Prioritise fast-absorbing protein and carbohydrates to kickstart recovery.

Meal Timing Tips

Consistency matters more than perfection. Try to eat 4-5 smaller meals throughout the day rather than 2-3 large ones. This keeps your metabolism active and prevents energy crashes. Never skip breakfast — it sets the tone for your energy levels all day.
